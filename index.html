Quick Facts

Did You Know?

01

The word tattoo comes from the Tahitian word tatau, introduced to Europe by Captain James Cook in 1769.

02

The oldest known tattooed human is Ötzi the Iceman, whose 61 tattoos are over 5,300 years old.

03

In Japan, traditional tebori tattooing is done by hand — each dot placed individually with a wooden or metal rod.

04

The first electric tattoo machine was patented by Samuel O'Reilly in 1891, adapted from Thomas Edison's engraving pen.
